About Us

Discover Your Paradise

Welcome to Baliraya Beach Resort

Baliraya Beach Resort is an exclusive beach house destination featuring pristine white sand and calm, crystal-clear waters. Located in the beautiful province of Davao Oriental, we offer our guests an unforgettable tropical paradise experience.

Our resort is strategically positioned near some of the region's most stunning natural attractions, including Oak Island, Waniban Island, and the famous Pujada Island. Just 30 kilometers from downtown Mati City, Baliraya Beach Resort provides the perfect balance of accessibility and seclusion.

With a commitment to excellence, we maintain our facilities to the highest standards, ensuring clean surroundings and a safe environment for all our guests.
