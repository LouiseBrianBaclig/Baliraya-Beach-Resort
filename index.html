The Baliraya Story

A Bali Dream,
Born on Mati's
Shores

There's a certain kind of quiet that only the sea can give you — a pause in the noise of the world where the only notification you receive is the gentle rhythm of waves kissing white sand.

Baliraya Beach Resort was born from one radical idea: what if the most beautiful escape from the city felt like a home the sea had always been keeping for you?

Tucked in the lush, tranquil shores of Barangay Lawigan — at the southeastern tip of Mati City, Davao Oriental — Baliraya is Davao's best-kept secret. Inspired by the soul-restoring serenity of Bali, it whispers of golden-hour reflections on white sand, open-air lanais, swaying palms, and the kind of stillness that genuinely resets you.

This is not just a resort. It's where Bali meets Mati — and where you finally exhale.

Moments You'll Carry Forever

This Is What Your Days —
and Nights — Feel Like

Words barely do it justice. But here's a glimpse of what's waiting for you at Baliraya.

☀️
01 — Dawn

Sunrise on Your Shoreline

You wake up before the world does. The air is cool and salt-kissed, the sky painted in shades of amber and rose. You step out to the lanai barefoot, coffee warming your hands, and watch as the sun lifts itself from the Philippine Sea. No alarm. No rush. No agenda. Just this — the most beautiful commute you've ever had.

🚣 02 — Adventure

Kayak for Two

A two-person kayak, an open sea, and the person you love most. Together you push through the gentle morning waves, leaving every worry behind on the shore. The only thing ahead of you is the horizon.

🔥
03 — Evening Magic

Bonfire Under the Stars

As the sun drops into the sea and the sky turns deep indigo, the bonfire crackles to life. Warm amber glow, the smell of salt and woodsmoke, the sound of laughter echoing down the shore — this is the evening that will come up in conversations for years. Someone tells a story. Everyone stays up far too late. Nobody minds.

🌊 04 — Stillness

Do Absolutely Nothing

The most profound Baliraya moments require no plans. You sink into the swing, the warm breeze carrying the scent of the ocean, and you simply breathe. Nowhere to be. Nothing to do. This is what rest actually feels like.

✨
05 — Night Sky

Stargazing from the Treehouse

Far from the glow of city lights, the Lawigan sky reveals itself as a different universe entirely. From the elevated perch of the Treehouse, the Milky Way stretches overhead in full, breathtaking clarity. You'll stay up far too late — and won't regret a single second. This is the kind of wonder that reminds you exactly why you needed to get away.

🎤 06 — Celebration

Your Private Concert Night

Stars out. Someone picks up the mic. Suddenly Baliraya becomes your own private concert venue — open sky, sea as your chorus, and a crowd of the people you love most. Belt it out. Nobody's judging here.
